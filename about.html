Biography

Jaleelah Giles is a passionate Psychologist and avid makeup artist known for her ability to captivate the beauty of a bride. Born and raised in a small town in Southeast Georgia, Jaleelah develpoed a love for makeup at a young age. Her curiosity and passion for makeup led her to embark on a journey to become an avid makeup artist, capturing moments of beauty and wonder along the way. Jaleelah's makeup looks reflect her uniqe ability to strengthen a person's facial features, highlighting the beauty and diversity and the intricacies of many different brides.

Outside of being a makeup artist, Jaleelah enjoys Psychology, singing, and finding insiration in nature and creativity. With a keen eye for detail, Jaleelah continues to push the boundries of her craft, sharing her pasion for makeup with others and inspiring them to see the world through different looks.
